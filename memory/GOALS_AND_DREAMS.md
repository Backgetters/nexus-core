## Success Metrics & KPIs

### Financial Metrics
- **Monthly Recurring Revenue (MRR):** Target $1,576 by month 3
- **Client Lifetime Value (CLV):** Track average client value over time
- **Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC):** Monitor cost per new client acquired
- **Profit Margins:** Track profitability across all service lines
- **Payment Channel Performance:** Revenue by payment method (PayPal, crypto)

### Operational Metrics
- **Client Acquisition Rate:** New clients per month
- **Client Retention Rate:** Percentage of clients continuing service
- **Service Delivery Success:** Percentage of services delivered on time
- **System Uptime:** 99%+ uptime for all automation systems
- **Response Time:** Client inquiry response within 24 hours

### Growth Metrics
- **Monthly Growth Rate:** Revenue and client growth percentage
- **Service Expansion Rate:** New services added per quarter
- **Market Penetration:** Percentage of target market reached
- **Competitive Position:** Market share and competitive advantage
- **Innovation Rate:** New capabilities and features implemented
