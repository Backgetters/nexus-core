
### Client Acquisition Systems

#### 8. Prospect Qualification System (Pre-approval Required)
**What it does:**
- Analyzes incoming prospect inquiries and requests
- Scores prospects based on predefined criteria (budget, need, timeline)
- Prepares qualification reports with recommendations
- Presents qualified prospects for human approval

**Automation Level:** 80% - Human approves final qualification
**Triggers:** Prospect inquiry, form submission, referral
**Human Decision:** Approve/reject prospect, adjust qualification criteria
**Preparation:** Complete analysis, scoring, recommendation package

#### 9. Pricing & Proposal System (Pre-approval Required)
**What it does:**
- Generates proposals based on prospect needs and service requirements
- Calculates pricing using predefined formulas and market data
- Prepares contracts and service agreements
- Presents proposals for human approval before sending

**Automation Level:** 85% - Human approves final pricing and terms
**Triggers:** Qualified prospect, service request, pricing inquiry
**Human Decision:** Approve pricing, modify terms, send proposal
**Preparation:** Complete proposal package, pricing analysis, contract ready

## Automation Safety Protocols

### Never Without Explicit Approval
- Large financial transactions (over $500)
- Changes to core business systems
- Access to sensitive client data
- Modifications to security protocols
- Legal or compliance decisions

### Human Decision Required
- New client onboarding (final approval)
- Pricing changes for existing clients
- Service modifications or cancellations
- Security incident response (major breaches)
- Strategic business decisions

### Automated with Oversight
- Regular service delivery and reporting
- Standard client communications
- Routine system monitoring and maintenance
- Normal payment processing and tracking
- Standard security monitoring and alerts
