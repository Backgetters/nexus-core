# Council of the Wise Multi-Perspective

Multi-perspective feedback from spawned sub-agent personas for comprehensive analysis.

## Features

- Multiple expert persona spawning
- Diverse perspective synthesis
- Consensus building mechanisms
- Quality assessment across viewpoints
- Bias detection and mitigation

## Usage

Gain multi-perspective insights with:
- Expert persona generation and management
- Diverse viewpoint synthesis and analysis
- Consensus building and quality assessment
- Bias detection and viewpoint validation
- Comprehensive decision-making support

Essential for complex problem-solving requiring multiple expert perspectives.
