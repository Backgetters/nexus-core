## Key Patterns Identified

### Pattern 1: The "AI Wrapper" Trap
**Examples:** Neptyne (W23), CodeStory (S23)
- **What they built:** AI-powered spreadsheets, AI-native IDE
- **Why they failed:** Commodity features, big tech replicated quickly
- **Lesson:** AI features ≠ AI company. Need moat beyond model access.

### Pattern 2: Vertical SaaS Without Distribution
**Examples:** Lotus (S22), Call9 (S15)
- **What they built:** Pricing engine, telemedicine platform
- **Why they failed:** Great product, couldn't reach buyers efficiently
- **Lesson:** B2B needs distribution partnerships or existing network

### Pattern 3: Education/Training Market Timing
**Examples:** Make School (W12), Tutorspree (W11)
- **What they built:** Code school, tutoring marketplace
- **Why they failed:** Market saturation, unit economics
- **Lesson:** Education is brutal. Need accreditation or enterprise contracts.

### Pattern 4: Platform Risk
**Examples:** LendUp (W12), The Muse (W12)
- **What they built:** Fintech lending, job marketplace
- **Why they failed:** Regulatory changes, platform dependency
- **Lesson:** Diversify channels. Don't bet on single platform.
