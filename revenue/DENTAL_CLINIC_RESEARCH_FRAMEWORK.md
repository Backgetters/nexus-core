## STEP-BY-STEP RESEARCH PROCESS

### Step 1: Google Search (2 minutes)
**Search 1:** "dental clinic Toronto downtown"
**Search 2:** "dentist Toronto midtown"

### Step 2: Extract Data (10 minutes)
For each clinic on page 1 of results:

| Field | Where to Find |
|-------|---------------|
| **Business Name** | Google result title |
| **Website** | Click website link |
| **Phone** | Google knowledge panel OR website contact page |
| **Email** | Website contact page OR info@website.com |
| **Address** | Google knowledge panel |
| **Google Rating** | Stars in Google result |

While on their website, check:
- [ ] Online booking system? (Y/N)
- [ ] Chatbot? (Y/N)
- [ ] Email newsletter signup? (Y/N)
- [ ] Mobile-friendly? (Test on your phone)
- [ ] Clear CTA? ("Book Now" / "Call Us")
