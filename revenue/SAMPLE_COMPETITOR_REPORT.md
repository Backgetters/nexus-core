## 💡 STRATEGIC INSIGHTS

### Pattern Detected: Platform Consolidation
Make.com and Zapier are racing to add AI. This threatens DIY tools but **helps** custom service providers because:
- Businesses try DIY, get frustrated, hire experts
- Complexity increases demand for done-for-you
- Our value prop = they don't have to learn new platforms

**Recommendation:** Create content targeting "tried DIY, need help" segment.

### Opportunity Gap: Industry-Specific Solutions
None of our competitors are specializing by industry. They're all generalist "we automate anything."

**Recommendation:** 
- Position as "Dental Practice Automation Specialist"
- Or "Legal Workflow Automation Expert"
- Own a vertical instead of competing horizontally

### Threat Warning: Price Compression
Agency X's $49 audit signals race to bottom. Two others rumored to be dropping prices.

**Recommendation:** 
- Don't compete on price
- Add value: "Audit + 3 Quick Wins Implementation" for $297
- Guarantee: "Find $10K+ in savings or money back"
